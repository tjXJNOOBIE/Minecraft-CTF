## Namespaces
* **Package names**: Verbose > Clean
    * Example `dev.tjxjnoobie.ctf.commands.admin` > `dev.tjxjnoobie.ctf.admin`
* **Class names**: Verbose > Simple
    * **Example**: `CTFSetFlagCommand` > `SetFlagCommand`
    * **Why**: This is a CTF command, not a generic set-flag handler. Precision prevents confusion.
